About This Report

This Gantt chart visualizes Work Orders from Manufacturing Orders, showing their scheduled dates, durations, status, and dependencies.

Status Colors
Entered - WO has been entered but not started
Started - WO is currently in progress
Fulfilled - WO has been completed
Interactions
  • Hover over a WO bar to see details (status, dates, duration, quantity)
  • Click on a WO bar to open the Manufacturing Order in Fishbowl
  • Use the Settings panel to filter by MO, change date range, sort, and adjust view scale
Dependencies

Arrows between WO bars indicate dependencies based on MOItem parent/child relationships. A dependent WO should start after its parent WO is completed.

Time Scales
  • Day - Shows individual days with hourly granularity
  • Week - Shows weeks with daily granularity
  • Month - Shows months with weekly granularity
Filters
  • Filter by MO - Show only WOs from a specific Manufacturing Order
  • Start Date - Adjust the visible date range
  • Sort - Order WOs by MO Number or Date Scheduled
